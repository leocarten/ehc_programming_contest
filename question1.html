Question 1: How deep was the treasure?

>> Captain Cypher had to dig deep in the ground to first find the treasure, as it had been buried! Captain Cypher is trying to figure out how far he dug to find his treasure. Imagining that Captain Cypher had started at exactly level 0 [ground level], calculate how far the treasure was buried if:
- The ; character represents 2 levels down.
- The . character represents 4 levels down.
- The % character represents 3 levels down.
>> For example:
- The string of ||||..% would represent 19 levels down.
- The string of .|% would represent 9 levels down.
>> Your puzzle input
[most likely a download-able txt file]
>> How many floors down was Captain Cypher's treasure?
Captain Cypher's treasure was levels down.
